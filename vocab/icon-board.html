Master Reference · Citane Visual Language

A complete map of all Citane icon categories. Each icon represents one part of the Buna journey — from the leaf growing on the plant to the cup served at the table, and from traditional knowledge to future possibilities. This board is the entry point to the full vocabulary.

Reference image — Citane Icon Board. All categories shown. Individual sections are documented in the pages below.

Our Icon Language

Each icon represents a part of the Citane journey — from leaf to cup, from tradition to future. The style is: Simple. Natural. Meaningful. Consistent. Made for our landscape. Icons are not decorative — they carry precise meaning and are used consistently across all Buna communications.

The Three Principles of the Icon Language

  • From leaf to landscape — every icon traces back to the plant and the place where it grows.
  • From tradition to innovation — the icon set holds both ancient practice and new knowledge without ranking one above the other.
  • From our fields to a better future — icons carry a direction of travel: better practices, better leaves, better outcomes.
