Buna Vocabulary

A Shared Language from Leaf to Cup

A common reference for farmers, processors, café staff, researchers, and anyone who works with Buna. Each section covers one part of the journey — with terms, descriptions, and connections between them.

Why this exists. Buna is a new category. Words borrowed from tea, coffee, or herbal medicine carry assumptions that do not always fit. This vocabulary builds a shared platform: one set of terms, agreed meanings, and consistent language across the farm, the processing shed, the café, and the conversation. It is a living document — terms will be refined as understanding grows.
