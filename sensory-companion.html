The companion can walk you through the library, the sensory experience of Buna, or the tools. Choose your path. No typing needed — everything is a tap.

About Buna: This library is built around Buna — a coffee leaf beverage from Ethiopian and Indonesian traditions, now being explored at Thumpassery Estate in Kerala. You don't need to know anything about it to begin.
