The Sensory Companion

A walk through
what you already sense

This is not a test. There are no correct answers. The companion will offer observations and ask what you notice. You choose where to go next.

A note on Buna: This school uses Buna — a coffee leaf beverage from the traditions of Ethiopia and Indonesia, now being explored at Thumpassery Estate in Kerala — as its primary example. You do not need to have tasted it. What you discover here applies to everything you eat and drink.
