Buna Library

Explorations of the Coffee Plant

Buna is a library of landscapes. Each project explores one part of the coffee plant and the worlds that emerge from it.

Coffee is larger than the roasted bean.


Seed. Fruit. Leaf.


These projects follow the coffee plant through experience, culture, processing, research, tradition, and possibility.

Fruit

Coffee Cherry Project

A landscape exploration of the coffee fruit: ripeness, mucilage, fermentation, drying, cascara, by-products, and the traces the fruit leaves behind.

Leaf

Coffee Leaf Library

The Buna Coffee Leaf Library of the Citane Project. A developing knowledge base for coffee leaves, processing, brewing, research, and climate-resilient possibilities.
