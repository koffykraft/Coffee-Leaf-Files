Cortisol Reduction: Stress Hormone Drop

How coffee leaf directly lowers your stress hormone (cortisol) over 45 minutes.

Blood Cortisol Levels

The drop: From 16.99 µg/dL to 7.19 µg/dL over 45 minutes. That's a 57% reduction in your main stress hormone.

Self-Reported Stress Level

How you feel: Drops from "very stressed" (11.67) to "calm" (6.08) in 30 minutes. That 46% shift matches the cortisol drop.

Processing Types: Which Has Most GABA?

GABA Content by Processing

Oolong-style and black (fermented) leaves have maximum GABA—0.40 mg/g. Green leaves have less (0.28 mg/g) but still effective.

Phenolic Content (Antioxidants)

Green and light fermented leaves have highest phenolic content (28–36 mg/g). Phenolics reduce oxidative stress—a key driver of anxiety.
